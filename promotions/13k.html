Pizza GoGo Giveaway Promotion Terms and Conditions

1. Promotion Period: The PizzaGoGo Giveaway Promotion ("Promotion") will run for a total of 13 weeks, starting from October 1st 2023 and ending on December 31st 2023.

2. Eligibility: To participate in the Promotion, you must be a resident of the United Kingdom with an address that falls in a participating stores delivery area and be 18 years of age or older at the time of entry.

3. Entry:

  • a. You will receive one free entry for every completed online order placed at pizzagogo.co.uk during the Promotion Period.
  • b. Each online order counts as one entry, regardless of its value. Cancelled orders will automatically cancel the corresponding entry to the giveaway.
  • c. Instructions for obtaining a free entry are provided here. Only one free entry per week is allowed.

4. Winner Selection: A random drawing will select one winner every Sunday from all eligible entries received during the previous seven days. The first winner will be drawn on October 8th, 2023. The winner is the person named on the Pizza GoGo account linked to the winning order number.

5. Winner Announcement: The winner's order number will be announced on Pizza GoGo's official social media channels by 10:00 AM each Sunday, along with an estimated arrival time to the winner's address. You must follow our social media channels to obtain the winning order number details and prepare for the "Winner Announcement Event" immediately following the draw. No prior arrangement will be made or notice provided to the winner by Pizza GoGo staff other than the announcement made on social media.

6. Prize: Each weekly winner will receive £1000 cash. To claim the prize, ensure your Pizza GoGo account information is accurate and matches your ID. If the ID doesn't match the account, the prize will be donated to a local charity chosen by Pizza GoGo Limited. Acceptable ID will be a Photo Driving License or Passport and Proof of Address.

7. Winner Announcement Event: Pizza GoGo's social media team will visit the address linked to the winning order number on the same day as the "Winner Announcement." The visit will occur immediately after the announcement, and the estimated arrival time will be provided via live stream on social media. If the winner is not present at the registered address upon arrival, the prize will be shared equally with the two neighbours on either side of the winners property. If only one neighbour is available they will win the entire prize amount or if no neighbours are available then the entire prize will be rolled over to the following week.
