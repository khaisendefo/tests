Allergen advice

Important notes

When ordering our FREE CHOICE pizza or POTATO SKINS + TOPPING, please refer to the individual toppings for allergen information.

Individual foods may come in contact with one another during preparation, which is not reflected in our document. Although efforts are made to avoid cross-contact of allergens, Pizza GoGo does not guarantee that cross-contact with allergens will not occur.

If you have any concerns please contact your store before ordering, and alert your phone operator if you or anyone in your party has a food allergy.
