Introduction

Pizza GoGo and Scratchmark have always respected the privacy of all visitors and users of our online services and games.
Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information. The following outlines our privacy policy.

We collect and use non-personally-identifiable data only as it might be needed for us to provide to you our Game and/or services (collectively, our "Services"). The Services are operated by Pizza GoGo and Scratchmark for all free users of the Game ("You").

In general, you may use the Game and visit the Game's related website pages (hereinafter, the "Website") without telling us who you are or revealing any personal information about yourself. We may track the Internet domain address from which people use internet services within the Game and analyze this data for trends and statistics, but individual users will remain anonymous unless they voluntarily tell us who they are. We collect via the Website only non-personally-identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request.

b. We have implemented security measures to protect the loss, misuse or alteration of information placed under our control and will ensure to safeguard this information at all times.
