Data safety & deletion

"GoGo Dash" game

Updated 26 March 2024.

THIS COVERS THE USE OF THE "GOGO DASH" GAME ONLY.

If you have any questions regarding your personal data, please contact our Data Protection Officer ("DPO") by emailing: data@pizzagogo.net



What information does GoGo Dash collect?

We do not collect personally-identifiable data from within the Game, however:
you may voluntarily submit a contact Email address to enter any running promotion, and:
we do anonymously track the Internet domain address (IP Address) from which people access the Game, and:
we do collect non-personally-identifying tags / initials (e.g. AAA) when you voluntarily enter them to our Services.
We do not collect personal data from publicly available sources and/or from third party data vendors.
We do not collect or store financial details (credit, debit, etc).
We do not have any connections whatsoever with social network services, such as Facebook.




Understanding your rights: Access, update, or delete your data

Your data belongs to you, and we want you to be in full control of what happens to your data. We have tried to make our Services easy to use and provide simple ways to update and delete your information. You can also contact our data protection officer by emailing data@pizzagogo.net and putting any request in writing. We will complete your request within 30 days.

If you make a request to delete your personal data and that data is necessary for the products or Services you have purchased, the request will be honoured only to the extent it is no longer necessary for any Services purchased or required for our legitimate business purposes or legal or contractual record keeping requirements.
