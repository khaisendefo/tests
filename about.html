About Pizza GoGo

A dream becomes a reality in 1987, with the birth of Pizza GoGo. During this period to this date the company has expanded rapidly with over 100 branches. We have been one of the UK's leading take-away and delivery companies and introduced the concept of free delivery!

It is a franchise operation and the success of Pizza GoGo is down to customer care, high quality products and attention to details - with our dough being made fresh everyday on the premises!

Take-away will continue to be an ever-growing business and home delivery has been big business in the UK and has certainly taken its percentage of the food market.

Delivery to your door

After introducing free delivery to the UK back in the 80's, we've made every attempt to continue this trend today.

However with constant rising costs some postcodes may incur a delivery surcharge - we'll let you know about these charges, if any, before you complete your order.
